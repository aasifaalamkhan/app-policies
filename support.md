# Support for KissMe.ai

**Welcome to the KissMe.ai Support Page!**

If you have any questions, feedback, or need assistance with the app, we are here to help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. How do I generate a video?**
Simply select "Single" or "Dual" mode, upload your photo(s), crop them, and tap "Generate". Your video will be ready in a few minutes.

**2. I purchased tokens but didn't receive them.**
Please try the "Restore Purchases" button in the Tokens screen. If that doesn't work, contact us with your order ID.

**3. What happens to my uploaded photos?**
Your privacy is our priority. Photos are uploaded strictly for video generation and are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours. We do not use them for training other models.

**4. How can I delete my account?**
Go to the **Profile** screen in the app and select "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your data and tokens.
